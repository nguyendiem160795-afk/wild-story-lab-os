# 🎬 Wild Story Lab OS

> Building a professional AI Animation Studio powered by Google Flow, Veo, and AI tools.

---

# Vision

Wild Story Lab is a production system for creating high-quality children's animated content with consistent characters, reusable assets, and standardized workflows.

Our goal is to build a scalable AI Animation Studio capable of producing educational and entertaining content for YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, and future digital products.

---

# Core Character

🐶 **Mochi**

Mochi is the flagship character of Wild Story Lab.

Every animation, story, and prompt must follow the official Mochi Character Bible to ensure visual and behavioral consistency.
