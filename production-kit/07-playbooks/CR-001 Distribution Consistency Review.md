# 1. Purpose

This document evaluates the consistency of Playbook OS.

Its purpose is to ensure that every Playbook follows the same structural standards, terminology, formatting conventions, lifecycle, and governance principles.

Consistency improves readability, reduces ambiguity, simplifies maintenance, and enables reliable execution by both humans and AI systems.

---

# 2. Review Scope

This review evaluates consistency across:

- Module structure
- Documentation format
- Naming conventions
- Terminology
- Playbook structure
- Metadata
- Workflow representation
- Versioning
- Quality standards

# 12. Identified Risks

Potential inconsistencies may arise from:

- Independent authoring styles
- Duplicate Playbooks
- Uncontrolled terminology
- Missing metadata

Mitigation:

- Standard templates
- Mandatory reviews
- Controlled vocabulary
- Governance approval

---

# 13. Recommendations

To preserve consistency:

- Maintain a centralized glossary.
- Use standard Playbook templates.
- Perform periodic consistency audits.
- Validate metadata automatically.
- Maintain naming governance.
