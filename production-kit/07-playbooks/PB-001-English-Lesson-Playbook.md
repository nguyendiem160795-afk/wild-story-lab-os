# PB-001 – English Lesson Playbook

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Owner:** Wild Story Lab

---

# Purpose

This playbook defines the complete production workflow for creating English learning videos using Google Flow (Veo 3.1).

The objective is to produce educational, entertaining, and highly reusable videos for children aged 3–8.

---

# Target Audience

- Age: 3–8 years
- Beginner English learners
- Parents
- Teachers

---

# Video Specifications

Duration:
- Shorts: 30–60 seconds
- Long-form: 3–8 minutes

Style:
- Pixar-quality 3D
- Bright colors
- Friendly animation
- Child-safe

---

# Production Pipeline

1. Select Topic
2. Select Vocabulary
3. Create Storyboard
4. Generate Prompt
5. Produce in Google Flow
6. Voiceover
7. Sound Effects
8. Export
9. Thumbnail
10. Upload to YouTube

---

# Lesson Structure

## Scene 1 – Hook

Capture attention in the first 3 seconds.

Example:

"Mochi discovers a mysterious giant apple!"

---

## Scene 2 – Introduce Vocabulary

Display the object clearly.

Voice:

"This is an APPLE."

---

## Scene 3 – Pronunciation

Repeat slowly.

APPLE

Apple

apple

---

## Scene 4 – Interaction

Ask the audience:

"Can you say APPLE?"

Pause for interaction.

---

## Scene 5 – Mini Challenge

Example:

Find the apple among three fruits.

---

## Scene 6 – Reward

Mochi celebrates.

Confetti.

Cheering.

---

## Scene 7 – Review

Repeat the word.

Show spelling.

Show pronunciation.

---

## Scene 8 – Goodbye

Mochi waves.

"See you next time!"

# Prompt Checklist

Before generating:

✓ Character consistency

✓ Correct vocabulary

✓ Child-friendly

✓ One clear action

✓ Bright lighting

✓ Clear camera movement
