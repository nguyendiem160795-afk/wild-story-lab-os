# 1. Purpose

Character Standard defines the official rules for creating, maintaining, and evolving all characters used in Wild Story Lab productions.

Its objectives are:

- Maintain visual consistency across all videos.
- Ensure reusable character assets.
- Standardize prompt construction.
- Support Google Flow (Veo 3.1) and future AI video tools.
- Enable scalable production for long-term content creation.

---

# 2. Scope

This standard applies to:

- Main characters
- Supporting characters
- Teachers
- Animals
- Mascots
- Temporary story characters

Every Character Bible must comply with this document.

---

# 3. Design Principles

## 3.1 Consistency First

A character must always be recognizable, regardless of scene, animation, or environment.

Core identity must remain unchanged.

---

## 3.2 Modular Design

Characters are built from reusable components instead of rewriting prompts from scratch.

Example modules include:

- Visual DNA
- Motion DNA
- Emotion DNA
- Voice DNA
- Prompt Blocks

---

## 3.3 Child-Friendly Design

All primary characters should be:

- Friendly
- Positive
- Expressive
- Easy to recognize
- Easy to animate
- Appealing to children aged 3–8

---

## 3.4 Future Scalability

The system must support hundreds of characters without changing the overall workflow.

Every new character follows the same document structure.
