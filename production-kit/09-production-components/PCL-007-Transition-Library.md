# Purpose

This document defines the standard transition language for Wild Story Lab OS.

Transitions connect scenes smoothly, guide viewer attention, and maintain storytelling rhythm across AI-generated videos.

# Transition Selection Guide

| Scene Change | Recommended Transition |
|--------------|------------------------|
| Classroom → Classroom | Cut |
| Lesson → Activity | Cross Dissolve |
| Story → Dream | Fade to White |
| Adventure | Whip Pan |
| Magic | Sparkle Burst |
| Bedtime | Fade Out |
| Ending | Slow Fade |

---

# Transition Duration

Fast

0.2–0.5 seconds

Medium

0.5–1.0 seconds

Slow

1.0–2.0 seconds

---

# Transition Rules

Always

- Support the story.
- Keep transitions smooth.
- Match transition speed with scene pacing.
- Use child-friendly effects.

Never

- Overuse flashy transitions.
- Change transition style every scene.
- Distract from the lesson.
- Use aggressive flashing effects.

---

# Best Practices

- Use simple cuts for dialogue.
- Reserve magical transitions for magical moments.
- Keep educational videos visually calm.
- Maintain consistency throughout the episode.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Excessive transition effects.
- Inconsistent transition timing.
- Transition unrelated to story context.
- Abrupt cuts during emotional scenes.
