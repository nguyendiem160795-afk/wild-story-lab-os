# Audio Selection Guide

| Scene | Recommended Audio |
|--------|-------------------|
| Greeting | Happy BGM + Cheerful Voice |
| Classroom | Soft Classroom Ambience |
| Reading | Calm Music + Page Turning |
| Cooking | Kitchen Ambience + Stirring |
| Adventure | Nature Ambience + Exciting BGM |
| Bedtime | Soft Lullaby + Crickets |

---

# Audio Rules

Always

- Keep dialogue clear.
- Balance music and narration.
- Match sound effects with on-screen actions.
- Use consistent volume levels.

Never

- Overpower narration with music.
- Layer too many sound effects.
- Use harsh or frightening sounds.
- Change music abruptly without story context.

---

# Best Practices

- Use recurring musical themes for brand recognition.
- Keep background music subtle during educational segments.
- Add sound effects only when they support the action.
- Maintain smooth audio transitions.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Music too loud.
- Missing ambient sound.
- Delayed sound effects.
- Repetitive sound loops.
- Mismatched emotion between music and visuals.
