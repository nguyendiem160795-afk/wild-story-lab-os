# Purpose

This document defines the standard facial expressions and emotional language used throughout Wild Story Lab OS.

The goal is to ensure every character communicates emotions clearly, consistently, and in a child-friendly way across AI video generation platforms.

Supported Platforms

- Google Flow
- Veo 3.1
- Imagen Video
- Runway
- Sora

---

# Expression Rules

Always

- Keep expressions readable.
- Match facial expression with body language.
- Maintain character identity.
- Use smooth emotional transitions.

Never

- Sudden emotion changes without story context.
- Overly exaggerated facial distortion.
- Scary or aggressive expressions in children's content.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Neutral face during emotional scenes.
- Mismatch between dialogue and expression.
- Switching emotions too quickly.
- Repeating the same smile in every scene.
