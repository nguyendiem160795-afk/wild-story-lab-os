# Purpose

This document defines the optimization profiles for supported AI video generation platforms.

Each platform interprets prompts differently. Platform Profiles ensure that prompts generated by Wild Story Lab OS are adapted to maximize quality, consistency, and generation success.

---

# Supported Platforms

- Google Flow
- Veo 3.1
- Runway
- Sora
- Imagen Video

---

# Platform Profile Structure

Every platform profile includes:

- Platform Overview
- Strengths
- Limitations
- Prompt Style
- Recommended Length
- Recommended Detail Level
- Optimization Rules
- Best Practices
- Common Mistakes
- Example Output

---

# Google Flow Profile

## Strengths

- Excellent character consistency
- Strong cinematic motion
- High-quality scene continuity
- Native integration with Veo

## Limitations

- Sensitive to conflicting instructions
- Better with structured prompts
- Limited image ingredient handling

## Prompt Style

Structured

Component-based

Clear hierarchy

---

## Recommended Prompt Length

250–500 words

---

## Detail Level

High

---

## Best Practices

- Use Character Bible references.
- Use standardized Camera Blocks.
- Limit one primary action per scene.
- Keep scene objectives explicit.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Too many simultaneous actions.
- Multiple lighting instructions.
- Repeating character descriptions.

---

# Veo 3.1 Profile

## Strengths

- Natural motion
- Excellent lighting
- Realistic physics
- High-quality animation

## Limitations

- Long prompts may dilute focus.
- Requires clear scene priorities.

---

## Recommended Prompt Length

180–400 words

---

## Detail Level

High

---

## Best Practices

- Prioritize motion.
- Define camera before action.
- Use one dominant emotion.
- Keep transitions simple.

---

# Runway Profile

## Strengths

- Fast generation
- Stylized visuals
- Flexible creative interpretation

## Limitations

- Lower character consistency.
- Requires concise prompts.

---

## Recommended Prompt Length

120–250 words

---

# Sora Profile

## Strengths

- Long-form scene understanding
- Complex environments
- Rich cinematic storytelling

## Limitations

- Requires highly structured prompts.
- Sensitive to ambiguous instructions.

---

## Recommended Prompt Length

300–600 words

---

# Imagen Video Profile

## Strengths

- Strong image quality
- Excellent color rendering
- High visual fidelity

## Limitations

- Less emphasis on dialogue.
- Benefits from visual-first descriptions.

# Profile Selection Rules

Choose Google Flow when:

- Character consistency is critical.
- Multi-scene educational videos.
- Pixar-style animation.

Choose Veo 3.1 when:

- Natural movement is the priority.
- High-quality cinematic visuals.

Choose Runway when:

- Fast iteration is needed.
- Experimental concepts.

Choose Sora when:

- Long-form storytelling.
- Rich cinematic environments.

Choose Imagen Video when:

- Image quality and visual fidelity are the highest priority.
