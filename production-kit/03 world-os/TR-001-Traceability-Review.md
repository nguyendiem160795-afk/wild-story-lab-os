# World OS Traceability Review

> Official traceability review document for Wild Story Lab World Operating System.

---

# 1. Purpose

This document evaluates the traceability system of World OS.

The purpose is to ensure that every world element can be tracked from its origin to final production usage.

Traceability provides:

- Source identification.
- Asset tracking.
- Version control.
- Production verification.
- World consistency protection.

---

# 2. Traceability Principle

Every world component must have a clear relationship chain.

The official traceability chain:

```
World Identity

        ↓

World Bible

        ↓

World Rules

        ↓

Locations

        ↓

Environment Assets

        ↓

AI Prompts

        ↓

Production Output

        ↓

QA Validation
```
