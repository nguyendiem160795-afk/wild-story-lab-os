# Distribution Traceability Review

> Official traceability review for the Wild Story Lab Distribution Operating System.

---

# 1. Purpose

This document validates the traceability of all distribution activities throughout the content lifecycle.

The objective is to ensure every published asset can be traced back to its production source, approval records, version history, and performance analytics.

---

# 2. Review Objectives

The traceability review verifies:

- Asset traceability
- Metadata traceability
- Publishing traceability
- Version traceability
- Analytics traceability
- Audit readiness
