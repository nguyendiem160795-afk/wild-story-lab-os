# Distribution OS Roadmap

> Official development roadmap for the Wild Story Lab Distribution Operating System.

---

# 1. Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term development strategy for Distribution OS.

The objective is to evolve Distribution OS into an intelligent, scalable, AI-assisted global content distribution platform.

---

# 2. Vision

Distribution OS aims to become:

```
Intelligent

↓

Automated

↓

Scalable

↓

Data-Driven

↓

Global
```

---

# 3. Strategic Goals

The roadmap focuses on five strategic objectives:

- Publishing Automation
- Platform Expansion
- Audience Growth
- Analytics Intelligence
- AI Optimization
