# RT-006 — Production Checklist

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Production

Owner: Wild Story Lab

Last Updated: 2026-07-23

---

# Purpose

Production Checklist is the final verification layer before a project is approved for publishing.

It consolidates validation results from every production module and ensures that the project meets Wild Story Lab quality standards.

---

# Objectives

- Verify production completeness
- Ensure cross-module consistency
- Reduce publishing errors
- Standardize production reviews
- Improve production quality
- Support repeatable workflows

---

# Checklist Workflow

Production Completed

↓

Collect Reports

↓

Review Critical Items

↓

Review Quality Metrics

↓

Approve or Reject

↓

Archive Results

↓

Ready for Publishing

# Approval Rules

## Approved

Requirements

- No critical issues
- QA passed
- Consistency score ≥ 90
- Prompt validation passed
- Render validation passed

---

## Conditional Approval

Requirements

- No critical issues
- Minor warnings only
- Improvement plan documented

---

## Rejected

Reasons

- Failed QA
- Failed consistency
- Missing Character Bible
- Missing render
- Missing educational objective

# Best Practices

- Complete every checklist before publishing.
- Archive all reports with the project.
- Record every production version.
- Never skip QA.
- Review failed items before re-rendering.
