# Story OS Architecture

> Official architecture definition for Wild Story Lab Story Operating System.

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the architecture of Story OS.

The purpose is to establish how story intelligence is created, managed, transformed, and delivered into production.

Story OS provides the narrative infrastructure for:

- Story creation.
- Episode development.
- Emotional design.
- Audience engagement.
- Scalable content production.

---

# 2. Architecture Philosophy

Story OS follows:

```
Create

↓

Structure

↓

Enhance

↓

Validate

↓

Produce
```

The system transforms creative ideas into repeatable storytelling assets.

---

# 3. Story OS Position

Story OS operates as the narrative intelligence layer.

Architecture relationship:

```
CHARACTER OS

Identity

        +

WORLD OS

        +

        ↓

STORY OS

Narrative Intelligence
```
