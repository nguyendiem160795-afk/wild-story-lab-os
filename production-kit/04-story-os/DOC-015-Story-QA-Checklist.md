# Story QA Checklist

> Official story quality assurance framework for Wild Story Lab Story Operating System.

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the final Story Quality Assurance Checklist.

The purpose is to validate that every story meets Wild Story Lab standards before production.

The QA system verifies:

- Story quality.
- Character consistency.
- World consistency.
- Emotional quality.
- Production readiness.

---

# 2. Story QA Definition

Story QA is:

```
A structured evaluation process

that confirms a story is complete,

consistent,

and production-ready.
```

---

# 3. QA Approval Flow

The approval process:

```
Story Draft

↓

Story Review

↓

QA Evaluation

↓

Approval

↓

Production
```
