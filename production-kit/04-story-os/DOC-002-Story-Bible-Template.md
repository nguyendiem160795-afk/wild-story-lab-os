# Story Bible Template

> Official story documentation template for Wild Story Lab Story Operating System.

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official Story Bible Template.

The Story Bible is the central reference document for every approved story.

It stores:

- Story identity.
- Narrative foundation.
- Character relationships.
- World connection.
- Emotional design.
- Expansion potential.

---

# 2. Story Bible Definition

A Story Bible is:

```
The complete identity document

of a story concept

from creation

to production.
```
