# Governance Terms (Thuật ngữ Quản trị)

> Official governance terminology dictionary for the Enterprise AI Operating System Specification (EAOSS).

---

# 1. Purpose (Mục đích)

This glossary defines governance-related terminology used throughout EAOSS.

The purpose is to establish a common language for:

- Decision-making.
- Responsibility management.
- Compliance.
- Control mechanisms.
- Organizational accountability.

---

# 2. Governance

## Vietnamese:
Quản trị

## Definition:

Governance is the framework of authority, responsibility, decision-making, and control used to guide and manage a system.

Governance defines:

- Who decides.
- Who owns.
- Who approves.
- How decisions are controlled.

---

# 3. Governance Model

## Vietnamese:
Mô hình quản trị

## Definition:

A Governance Model defines the structure, roles, processes, and relationships used to operate governance activities.

Example:

```text
Strategic Governance
        ↓
Architecture Governance
        ↓
Technical Governance
        ↓
Operational Governance
```

---

# 4. Authority

## Vietnamese:
Thẩm quyền

## Definition:

Authority is the formal right to make decisions, approve actions, or control resources.

Example:

An Architecture Board has authority to approve architecture decisions.

---

# 5. Responsibility

## Vietnamese:
Trách nhiệm

## Definition:

Responsibility is the duty assigned to a person, role, or system to perform specific activities.

Example:

Owner responsibility:

- Maintain artifact.
- Update documentation.
- Ensure accuracy.

---

# 6. Accountability

## Vietnamese:
Trách nhiệm giải trình

## Definition:

Accountability means being answerable for decisions, actions, and outcomes.

Difference:

```text
Responsibility = Doing the work

Accountability = Answering for the result
```

---

# 7. Ownership

## Vietnamese:
Quyền sở hữu

## Definition:

Ownership identifies the person or role responsible for maintaining and governing an artifact or capability.

Owner responsibilities:

- Quality.
- Maintenance.
- Evolution.
- Lifecycle decisions.

---

# 8. Decision Rights

## Vietnamese:
Quyền quyết định

## Definition:

Decision Rights define who has authority to make specific types of decisions.

Example:

| Decision | Authority |
|-|-|
| Document Update | Owner |
| Policy Change | Governance Authority |
| Architecture Change | Architecture Review |

---

# 9. Governance Authority

## Vietnamese:
Cơ quan quản trị

## Definition:

A Governance Authority is a person, group, or body responsible for approving and controlling governed activities.

Examples:

- Architecture Board.
- Review Committee.
- Governance Owner.

---

# 10. Control

## Vietnamese:
Kiểm soát

## Definition:

A Control is a mechanism used to ensure activities follow requirements, standards, and policies.

Examples:

- Review.
- Approval.
- Audit.
- Validation.

---

# 11. Governance Rule

## Vietnamese:
Quy tắc quản trị

## Definition:

A Governance Rule is a mandatory requirement established to guide behavior and decisions.

Example:

"All production changes require approval."

---

# 12. Compliance

## Vietnamese:
Tuân thủ

## Definition:

Compliance is the condition of satisfying defined governance requirements.

Compliance may include:

- Standards.
- Policies.
- Regulations.
- Reviews.

---

# 13. Non-Compliance

## Vietnamese:
Không tuân thủ

## Definition:

Non-Compliance occurs when an activity, artifact, or decision violates approved requirements.

Examples:

- Missing review.
- Unauthorized change.
- Invalid version.

---

# 14. Audit

## Vietnamese:
Kiểm toán / Kiểm tra truy vết

## Definition:

Audit is a systematic examination of activities, records, and controls to verify compliance and effectiveness.

Audit evaluates:

- Evidence.
- Process.
- Decisions.
- Outcomes.

---

# 15. Review

## Vietnamese:
Đánh giá

## Definition:

Review is a structured evaluation activity performed before approval or publication.

Examples:

- Architecture Review.
- Security Review.
- Quality Review.

---

# 16. Approval

## Vietnamese:
Phê duyệt

## Definition:

Approval is the formal authorization that confirms an artifact, decision, or change is accepted.

---

# 17. Exception

## Vietnamese:
Ngoại lệ

## Definition:

An Exception is an approved deviation from an established requirement.

Exception requires:

- Reason.
- Impact assessment.
- Approval.
- Tracking.

---

# 18. Risk

## Vietnamese:
Rủi ro

## Definition:

Risk is the possibility that an event may negatively affect objectives, quality, security, or operations.

---

# 19. Mitigation

## Vietnamese:
Giảm thiểu rủi ro

## Definition:

Mitigation is an action taken to reduce the likelihood or impact of a risk.

---

# 20. Governance Maturity

## Vietnamese:
Mức độ trưởng thành quản trị

## Definition:

Governance Maturity describes the capability level of governance practices.

Levels may include:

```text
Initial
 ↓
Defined
 ↓
Managed
 ↓
Optimized
```
