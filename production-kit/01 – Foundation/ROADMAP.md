# Foundation Roadmap (Lộ trình Phát triển Nền tảng)

> Strategic roadmap for the evolution of the Foundation module.

---

# 1. Purpose (Mục đích)

This roadmap defines the long-term development plan for the Foundation module.

Its objective is to ensure that the architectural, engineering, governance, and documentation principles of EAOSS evolve in a controlled and consistent manner.

---

# 2. Vision (Tầm nhìn)

Build a comprehensive foundation library that serves as the authoritative source of engineering knowledge for every EAOSS module.

The Foundation module should become the single source of truth for:

- Architecture
- Engineering
- Documentation
- Governance
- Standards
- Policies

---

# 3. Strategic Goals (Mục tiêu chiến lược)

The Foundation roadmap focuses on:

- Expanding principle libraries.
- Defining engineering standards.
- Establishing governance policies.
- Improving architectural consistency.
- Supporting future EAOSS modules.

---

# 4. Development Phases

Objective:

Create the essential architectural documents.

Deliverables:

**Completed**

---

# 5. Long-Term Objectives

The Foundation module should eventually provide:

- Complete engineering handbook
- Enterprise documentation standards
- Architectural guidance
- Repository governance
- Knowledge reference for AI engineering

---

# 6. Success Criteria

The roadmap is considered fulfilled when:

- All principle documents are completed.
- Standards library is established.
- Governance policies are documented.
- Foundation is referenced by every EAOSS module.
- Architectural consistency is maintained across the repository.

---

# 7. Risks

Potential risks include:

- Inconsistent terminology
- Duplicate principles
- Architectural drift
- Documentation fragmentation

Mitigation:

- Centralized governance
- Standard reviews
- Cross-module traceability
- Periodic architectural audits

---

# 8. Dependencies

The Foundation roadmap depends on:

- Module 00 – Master
- Repository governance
- EAOSS review process

All downstream modules depend on the successful completion of the Foundation roadmap.
