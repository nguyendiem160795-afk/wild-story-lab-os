# Planning System Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap defines the strategic evolution of the Planning System within the Enterprise AI Operating System Specification (EAOSS).

The roadmap provides a structured view of future enhancements while preserving architectural stability, backward compatibility, governance compliance, and enterprise interoperability.

---

# Vision

The Planning System shall evolve into an intelligent enterprise planning platform capable of:

- Autonomous planning
- Adaptive replanning
- Multi-agent coordination
- Predictive planning
- Enterprise-scale optimization
- Explainable decision planning
- Continuous learning
- Cross-domain orchestration

---

# Strategic Objectives

Future development shall prioritize:

- Planning intelligence
- Enterprise scalability
- Governance automation
- Optimization efficiency
- Security enhancement
- Explainability
- Operational resilience
- AI-assisted planning
