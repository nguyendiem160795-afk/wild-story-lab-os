# KIT-001 – Project Folder Template

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Owner:** Wild Story Lab

---

# Purpose

Standard folder structure for every Wild Story Lab video project.

Every production must follow this structure to ensure consistency and easy collaboration.

---

# Folder Structure

Project_Name/

├── 01_Brief/

│   └── References/
│
├── 02_Storyboard/
│   ├── Storyboard.md
│   └── Scene_Notes.md
│
├── 03_Prompts/
│   ├── Scene_01.txt
│   ├── Scene_02.txt
│   ├── ...
│   └── Scene_08.txt
│
├── 04_Flow/
│   ├── Flow_Project_Link.md
│   ├── Render_01.mp4
│   └── Render_02.mp4
│
├── 05_Audio/
│   ├── Voice/
│   ├── Music/
│   └── SFX/
│
├── 06_Thumbnail/
│   ├── Thumbnail.psd
│   ├── Thumbnail.png

│
├── 07_SEO/
│   ├── Title.md

├── 08_Final/
