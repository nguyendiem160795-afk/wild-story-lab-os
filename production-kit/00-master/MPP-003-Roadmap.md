Version: 1.0.0

Status: Active

# Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term development strategy for Wild Story Lab OS.

It provides a structured plan for building a scalable AI-powered content production system for YouTube, Google Flow, Veo, and future AI creative tools.

---

# Vision

Build the world's most complete AI Production Operating System for creating educational animated content.

---

# Core Objectives

- Standardize production workflows.
- Maintain character consistency.
- Improve production speed.
- Ensure quality control.
- Enable large-scale content creation.
- Support future automation.
