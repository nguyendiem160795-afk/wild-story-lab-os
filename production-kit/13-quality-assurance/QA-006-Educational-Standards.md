# Purpose

Educational Standards defines the quality requirements for all educational content produced within Wild Story Lab OS.

It ensures every lesson is accurate, age-appropriate, engaging, and aligned with the project's learning objectives.

---

# Objectives

- Maintain educational accuracy
- Improve learning effectiveness
- Ensure age appropriateness
- Support child-safe content
- Standardize educational quality
- Enable measurable learning outcomes

# Common Issues

Critical

- Incorrect educational content
- Wrong pronunciation
- Unsafe content
- Inappropriate vocabulary

Major

- Lesson too difficult
- Weak reinforcement
- Poor pacing

Minor

- Excess repetition
- Minor pronunciation variation
- Slightly advanced vocabulary

# Best Practices

- Teach one primary concept per lesson.
- Reinforce key vocabulary naturally.
- Combine spoken language with visuals.
- Encourage curiosity instead of memorization.
- End with a positive review or celebration.
