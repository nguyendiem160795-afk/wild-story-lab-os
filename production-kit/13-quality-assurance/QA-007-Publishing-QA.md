Version: 1.0.0

---

# Purpose

Publishing QA defines the validation standards required before any production is released to public platforms.

It ensures every published video meets branding, SEO, technical, legal, educational, and platform-specific requirements.

---

# Objectives

- Standardize publishing quality
- Improve discoverability
- Protect brand consistency
- Maximize audience engagement
- Reduce publishing errors
- Support multi-platform deployment
