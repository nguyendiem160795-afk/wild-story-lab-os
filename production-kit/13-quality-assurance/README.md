# Purpose

Quality Assurance (QA) defines the standards, validation procedures, scoring systems, and approval workflow required before any Wild Story Lab production can be released.

The QA module ensures that every project meets consistent standards for storytelling, character continuity, educational value, technical quality, and publishing readiness.

---

# Objectives

- Standardize production quality
- Detect production issues early
- Ensure character consistency
- Validate educational content
- Improve publishing readiness
- Support automated quality control

# QA Modules

## QA-001 Final Video Checklist

Final production verification before release.

---

## QA-002 Character Consistency

Validate character appearance, personality, voice, clothing, and behavior.

---

## QA-003 Story Validation

Validate story structure, pacing, emotional flow, and educational objectives.

---

## QA-004 Prompt Quality

Evaluate prompt completeness, platform optimization, and consistency.

---

## QA-005 Render Validation

Verify technical rendering quality, resolution, lighting, motion, and camera work.

---

## QA-006 Educational Standards

Ensure learning objectives, vocabulary accuracy, and child-friendly content.

---

## QA-007 Publishing QA

Validate metadata, thumbnails, SEO, branding, and publication settings.

---

## QA-008 QA Scoring System

Calculate production scores and determine release approval.

# Success Criteria

A production is considered release-ready when

- All mandatory QA checks pass.
- Production score meets the minimum threshold.
- No critical issues remain unresolved.
- Final approval is recorded.
